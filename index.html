Domains

Internet of things

Internet of things is the mass communication and interconnectivity of multiple devices through the internet. With the help of various sensors we can send information to devices which can then be executed as per the instructions.

Augmented Reality

Augmented reality is the technology of presenting animations in the real world in real time. The world of the user is digitally "augmented" to provide the experience.

Machine Learning

Teaching a computer how to do a task or to predict an outcome is done with the help of machine learning. With the help of various algorithms the machine can learn itself without the need of help by humans.
